Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 12, 2026 · Effective: January 1, 2026

ParcoursCA is built on the principle of privacy by default. Your data stays on your device. We never sell your personal information to third parties.

1. Who We Are

ParcoursCA is a Canadian social integration support platform, developed and operated by Fjord Tech Finance, a company based in Quebec, Canada, doing business under the trade name ParcoursCA. The application is distributed on the Apple App Store and Google Play Store by Organisme d'Intervention Communautaire Spécialisée (OCIS), under an authorization agreement with Fjord Tech Finance.

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at: support@parcoursca.ca

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide our services:

  • Profile information: name, province of residence, immigration status (optional)
  • Language preference and accessibility settings
  • Anonymized usage data (features used, login frequency)
  • Email address for government notifications and alerts
  • Tax and government documents you choose to store (locally encrypted)

We never collect: full social insurance numbers, banking data, or biometric data.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Personalize your dashboard based on your province and situation
  • Send you alerts about government benefits you may be eligible for
  • Improve our services through aggregated, anonymized usage data
  • Contact you about important updates to your account
  • Comply with our legal obligations in Canada

4. Data Storage and Security

Your documents and sensitive data are locally encrypted on your device using the AES-256-GCM algorithm. Our servers never store your documents in plain text.

Data transmitted to our servers is protected by TLS 1.3. Your database (profile, documents, history) is hosted exclusively in Canada — AWS ca-central-1 region via Supabase. Peripheral services (Stripe payments, OneSignal notifications, Anthropic AI assistant) transit through US-based servers, without permanent storage of your personal data. See section 5 for details on each sub-processor.

5. Third-Party Sharing and Sub-processors

We never sell, rent, or share your personal data for commercial purposes. We use the following technical sub-processors, each bound by a strict confidentiality agreement:

  • Supabase — database and authentication. AWS hosting (North America). Privacy Policy
  • Stripe — secure payment processing. US-based, PCI-DSS Level 1 certified. Your card data never passes through our servers. Privacy Policy
  • OneSignal — push notification delivery. US-based. Notifications are only sent with your explicit consent. Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic — AI assistant request processing. US-based. Conversations are not retained beyond the session. Privacy Policy
  • Vercel — parcoursca.ca website hosting. US-based, North American servers. Privacy Policy

We may also share information to comply with a legal obligation or Canadian court order, or with your explicit consent (e.g., consultation with an accounting partner).

6. Your Rights

Under PIPEDA and Quebec's Law 25, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct any inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Withdraw your consent to data processing
  • Receive your data in a portable format
  • File a complaint with the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada

To exercise these rights: support@parcoursca.ca

7. Cookies and Similar Technologies

Our marketing site (parcoursca.ca) uses only essential session cookies. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party trackers. Our application (app.parcoursca.ca) does not place any cookies.

8. Changes to This Policy

We will notify you of any significant changes by email or in-app notification at least 30 days before they take effect. The update date always appears at the top of this page.
